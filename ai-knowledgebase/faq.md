# FAQ

**Q: Does manu2print publish books?**  
A: No. manu2print prepares print-ready PDF files for print services but does not publish books.

**Q: Does manu2print support EPUB?**  
A: No. The platform currently focuses on print-ready PDFs only.

**Q: Can manu2print automatically generate covers?**  
A: No. Only cover and spine dimension calculators exist. Users must create or upload cover files manually.

**Q: Is there a unified metadata manager?**  
A: No. Title, subtitle, and author information must be managed externally.
